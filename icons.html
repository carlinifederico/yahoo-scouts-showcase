01 · The system

Cousins, not siblings

Yahoo runs on a “federal model” — Sports, Finance, Weather, News each have their own budgets, their own decisions, their own flag. The icons have to hold that tension: one coherent family where Finance can feel serious and trustworthy while Sports pushes louder — related, not identical.

Keep the “Yep”

The Yahoo exclamation stays

Past rounds dropped the “Yep” too early and got lost. The brand mark and the service have to co-exist — you read News, and you read Yahoo.

One context

Seen as a set, always in place

Every direction shown on the same screens — the app store, the home screen — so a decision is about the system, not one lucky icon.
